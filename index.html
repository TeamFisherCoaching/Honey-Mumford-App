Why it matters in coaching

  • Self‑reflection for coachees: spot blind‑spots and play to strengths when tackling new challenges.
  • Coaching agility: adapt your questions, feedback and practice tasks to each individual’s dominant style.
  • Balanced development: surface the ‘less‑used’ quadrants so learners grow in all modes.
